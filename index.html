A small desert village nestled in the dunes' hollow, Cabo Polonio is a unique place, one of those rare magic oases from the other side of the world, planted in the middle of nowhere. Protected from the tingling of civilization, Cabo Polonio is a nature reserve that is difficult to access, the charms of which have been preserved from the infernal mechanisms of tourism. Here, only the songs of the birds, the cries of the sea lions and the murmur of the waves remain to remind you of the world.
